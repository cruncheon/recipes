Garlic Butter Toast

Description

Easy toast topped with garlic and butter.

Ingredients

  • Bread Roll
  • Grounded Garlic
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a bowl using the microwave.
  2. Add garlic to butter and stir.
  3. Slice bread.
  4. Toast bread.
  5. Top toast with the garlic butter using a brush.
  6. Serve.
