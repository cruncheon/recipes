Ground Beef Tomato Sauce Spaghetti

Description

A tasty and easy spaghetti dinner.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti noodles

Steps

  1. Oil and warm pan.
  2. Brown beef.
  3. Warm sauce in pan.
  4. Put meat and sauce to the side.
  5. Bring water to boil in pot.
  6. Boil noodles for 7 minutes.
  7. Strain noodles.
  8. Serve.
