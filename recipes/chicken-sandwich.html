Lemon Pepper Chicken Submarine Sandwich

Description

A delicious deli quality lemon pepper chicken submarine sandwich made easily at home.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Lemon Pepper Mix
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pietro Dressing
  • Submarine Sandwich Bread

Steps

  1. Slice chicken breasts
  2. Marinate chicken in lemon pepper mix
  3. Prep lettuce
  4. Prep carrots
  5. Prep onions
  6. Pan cook chicken
  7. Slice bread almost in half, leaving a hinge
  8. Mayo bread
  9. Place lettuce
  10. Add chicken
  11. Top with carrots and onions
  12. Drizzle with Pietro dressing
  13. Cut and serve
